About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Ben Grant and I am a growing Ottawa photographer and graphic designer. I am still exploring the many sides of photography. I love taking a variety of different pictures, experimenting with the many different sides and I have a growing passion for photography. One side I really want to grow in is portraits and to explore that side to eventually make money. I am excited to share my journey with many of you and see where I take my photography. Come along and enjoy the ride :)
